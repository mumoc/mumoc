# The Shark Effect

Fishermen sometimes place a shark inside fish tanks during transport.

Without the shark:

- fish stop moving
- the system becomes stagnant
- fish become unhealthy

With the shark:

- fish keep moving
- the ecosystem remains active

## In Teams

The shark represents a person who introduces constructive tension.

This person:

- asks difficult questions
- challenges assumptions
- pushes for clarity
- prevents complacency

The goal is not conflict.

The goal is movement.

Healthy tension keeps teams alive and learning.
