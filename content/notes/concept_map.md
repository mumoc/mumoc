# Concept Map

This document maps the core ideas behind my work as a **Team Architect**.

The goal is to organize concepts so they can evolve into blog posts, talks, and frameworks.

---

## Core Framework

**Care to Deliver**

A method for restoring trust, alignment, and movement in teams.

Steps:

1. Observe human signals
2. Evaluate agreements
3. Evaluate evaluations
4. Evaluate collaboration
5. Introduce movement
6. Encourage sharing
7. Build trust → unlock teamwork

---

## Core Philosophy

Empathy
Compassion
Sharing
Trust

These values create environments where teams can collaborate safely.

Related concepts:

- Sharing is Caring
- Psychological Safety
- Trust and Accountability

## Team Dynamics

Concepts related to how teams behave and evolve.

- Isolation vs Teamwork
- Team Stagnation
- The Shark Effect
- Psychological Safety

These concepts explain why teams sometimes stop moving and how movement can be restored.

## Outcomes

Healthy systems produce outcomes that seem extraordinary.

Key idea:

- Improbable Outcomes

These are not forced results, but emergent properties of strong collaboration.

## Future of Work

The evolution of collaboration between humans and artificial intelligence.

Key idea:

- AI Peer Collaboration

Humans and AI working as cognitive teammates rather than tool users.
