# Isolation vs Teamwork

Teams fail when individuals become isolated.

Isolation happens gradually and often goes unnoticed.

## Signs of Isolation

- private conversations dominate communication
- people hesitate to ask questions publicly
- knowledge is hoarded
- collaboration decreases

Over time, individuals behave as separate units rather than a team.

## Restoring Connection

Small actions can reconnect teams.

Examples include:

- encouraging public discussions
- thanking people for asking questions
- sharing context openly
- creating safe spaces for dialogue

Connection leads to sharing.

Sharing leads to trust.

Trust leads to teamwork.
