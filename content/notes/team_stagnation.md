# Team Stagnation

Teams rarely fail suddenly.

They slowly stop moving.

Stagnation occurs when learning, experimentation, and collaboration decrease.

## Signs of Stagnation

- silent communication channels
- minimal collaboration
- hollow rituals
- meetings focused on reporting instead of progress
- blockers remaining unresolved

## Causes

Common causes include:

- lack of trust
- unclear expectations
- broken communication
- poor metrics
- fear of failure

## Restoring Movement

Stagnation can be reversed by introducing movement.

Movement exposes hidden issues and forces teams to confront reality.

Examples include:

- restructuring collaboration
- surfacing blockers
- shifting focus toward outcomes
- encouraging open discussion
