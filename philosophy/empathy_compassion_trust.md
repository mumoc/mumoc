# Empathy, Compassion, and Trust

Strong teams are built on human understanding.

## Empathy

Empathy is the ability to understand another person's perspective, context, and emotions.

It allows leaders and teammates to see beyond surface behaviors and recognize underlying needs and concerns.

## Compassion

Compassion goes one step further.

It is not only understanding others, but creating conditions where they can:

- improve
- learn
- fail safely
- grow

Compassion turns understanding into action.

## Trust

Trust emerges when people feel safe sharing openly.

Without trust:

- collaboration becomes forced
- communication becomes defensive
- innovation slows down

With trust:

- teams experiment
- people ask questions
- improvement loops emerge naturally
