# Sharing is Caring

Sharing is one of the most powerful forces in teamwork.

When people share openly, knowledge flows and collaboration becomes possible.

## What Sharing Means

Sharing is not only about information.

It includes sharing:

- ideas
- context
- uncertainty
- mistakes
- credit
- responsibility

Sharing requires vulnerability.

And vulnerability creates trust.

## The Chain

Empathy + Compassion
↓
Sharing
↓
Vulnerability
↓
Trust
↓
Teamwork
↓
Improbable Outcomes

## Why Teams Fail

Most teams struggle because information is hoarded or filtered.

When people isolate knowledge, the team loses collective intelligence.

Sharing restores the system.
